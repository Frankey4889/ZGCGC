Our Story

Built on the Rock of God's Word

Zion Gospel Church of Grace and Mercy was established on the unshakeable foundation of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We are a Spirit-filled, Bible-believing community called to preach the good news, make disciples, and serve our generation with love.

From our very first gathering, the vision has been clear: every person who walks through our doors should encounter the living God — and leave transformed. Whether you are a long-time believer or seeking for the very first time, you are welcome here.
